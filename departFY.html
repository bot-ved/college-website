First Year

About Department

First year Bachelor of Engineering Department plays a vital role in overall development of students towards Engineering Courses. The subjects which taught in first year are Engineering Chemistry, Engineering Physics, Engineering Mathematics, Engineering Mechanics, Communication Skill, Social Skill, Engineering Drawing, Electrical Engineering and Computer Programming. These subjects are divided in two groups.
